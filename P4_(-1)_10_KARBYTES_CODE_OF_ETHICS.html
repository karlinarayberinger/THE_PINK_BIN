KARBYTES_CODE_OF_ETHICS

The following precepts briefly summarize the ethical priorities endorsed by the author of this website. (23_MARCH_2022)

Rules of Life:

1. Avoid complaining.

2. Appreciate what is enjoyable about existence.

3. Accept reality exactly as it is.

4. Live as though already dead (or soon to be dead).

5. Be as impersonal as nature.

6. Maximize self reliance.
